Web Community Leads UK and IE


Web Community Leads UK and IE is a network of people who run meet up groups or simular events focused around web technologies.
It exists to help event organisers to share resouces and ask for advice when needed.

How to get involved

  1. Read though our Code of Conduct .
  2. Visit our Slack. Currently it is invite only, you'll have to track down someone already involved and ask them to add you.
  3. Check out all the Infomation and Resources our community has compiled on running events.
